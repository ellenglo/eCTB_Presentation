What is eCTB Promo?

An easy way to provide external e-book gifting with DRM using a promo code or QR code distributed on physical cards or e-cards

What is it for?

Giveaway at conferences

What is next?

  • Provide galleys
  • Promo sweepstakes, stickers, scratch-offs
  • General gifting: authors, reviewers, professors
  • Bundles: Buy e-book and gift print OR buy print and gift e-book

Why eCTB Promo?

  • It saves money!
  • Provides e-book fulfillment for HC
  • Fulfills contract obligations
